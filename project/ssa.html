SinggahSiniAja is a leading travel agency specializing in fast boat services and island-hopping adventures in Bali and its surrounding islands. SinggahSiniAja is an official partner with Ekajaya Fast Ferry, which is one of the best companies that provide reliable and comfortable fast boat transportation services from Bali to Gili island, and various islands around Bali and Lombok.
In this project, I worked mostly on the backend. Creating Restful API using Laravel, created some test cases, and also created mockups with Bootstrap and Blade Template to test the API.
