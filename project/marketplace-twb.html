Twibbonize Marketplace
Project : Internship Project
Build with : Strapi, React, Tailwind, Mysql

Twibbonize Marketplace is a website that connects talented individuals with strong design skills to creators who are either confused or lack the time to prepare their campaign pages. Through Twibbonize Marketplace, these talented individuals assist in making campaign pages visually appealing.
Currently, the assistance provided by talent on the Twibbonize Marketplace includes creating Frames, Posters, and Thumbnail Designs. All three are crucial elements accompanying a campaign to ensure that campaign supporters can comprehend it.
My role in this project is as a Backend Developer who develops APIs, Authentication and Authorization issues, as well as a person in charge of communicating with the Frontend team.
