Visit this repo
Point of Sale Website
Project : Bootcamp Final Project
Build with : Laravel, Bootstrap, Mysql

Tokoku is a simple yet powerful Point of Sale (POS) website designed to streamline and enhance various aspects of your business operations. From transaction management to inventory tracking and customer relations, Tokoku is a valuable tool aimed at boosting your business's efficiency and minimizing costly errors in bookkeeping and reducing losses due to unsold or expired goods.
Tokoku is the culmination of my skills and knowledge acquired during a 6-month bootcamp at Hacktivate. It showcases the use of advanced technology and industry-standard practices in business management and web development.
