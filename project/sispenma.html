Sispenma

Sistem Penjadwalan Belajar Mengajar(Sispenma) is a website used to organize lesson schedules and teaching and learning activities.

Visit this repo
Fullstack Laravel Project
Undergraduate Thesis

Sispenma is a web-based application that I developed as a requirement for my undergraduate degree. This revolutionary application, built using the Laravel framework, aims to address the frequent scheduling conflicts that often occur in schools. Short for "Sistem Penjadwalan Belajar Mengajar," Sispenma utilizes intelligent Ant Colony Optimization algorithms to automatically optimize the scheduling of classes.
Sispenma can tackle several complex challenges, such as preventing schedule conflicts among teachers, classrooms, and subjects. It also ensures the efficient utilization of school resources, maximizing the time and energy of educators and students.
With Sispenma, schools can plan better schedules, improve efficiency in the teaching and learning process, and reduce the frustration caused by scheduling mismatches.
