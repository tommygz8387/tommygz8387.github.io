Online Store Website Mockup
Project :Freelance Project
Build With : Laravel, Bootstrap, Mysql

Singgahsini is an online store that offers a wide range of homemade herbal products. This website has been created with the purpose of supporting Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (UMKM) and various partners who wish to sell and market their products.
This platform is designed to empower local businesses and entrepreneurs by providing them with a digital marketplace to showcase and sell their homemade herbal products. From natural remedies to wellness products, Singgahsini aims to be a hub for authentic, homemade herbal items that cater to a diverse range of preferences and needs.
The website I have developed is essentially a mockup, serving as a preliminary representation of the website that will be further developed in the future. It also serves as a sample that can be presented to potential investors, in order to secure the necessary funding for the further development of this application.
